Book Me Restaurant

Book Me Restaurant was made to minimize problems in table booking process, specially in holidays and special occasions.

Team role

There were 2 parts: research and design. The research part was team work of 3, and the design part was made by me.

Research

Our team conducted user survey on 50 people to analyse and summerize customers behavior on rautaurant booking. The customer aged from 18 to 34, usualy go with group of 2-4 people. What attracted them most to place a booking was that they can have promotion.
